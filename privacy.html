Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 28, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Balbi Token collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you interact with the Service:

  • Wallet Address: Collected to send tokens and to enforce the cooldown period between claims.
  • Last Claim Timestamp: Stored to track the cooldown period and prevent abuse of the Service.

2. Use of Cookies and Local Storage

The site uses local storage in your browser to verify consent to this policy. This token is a simple marker indicating that you have accepted the policy.

Your private key is not stored on our servers. It is only used locally in your browser to sign transactions and is never transmitted to our backend infrastructure.

3. Purpose of Data Processing

The collected data (wallet address and timestamp) is used exclusively to ensure the fair operation of the Service, distributing tokens equitably and preventing spam or abuse.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect collected information against unauthorized access. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights (LGPD)

According to the Brazilian General Data Protection Law (LGPD), you have the right to:

  • Access the information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of incomplete or outdated data.
  • Request anonymization, blocking, or deletion of unnecessary data or data processed in violation of the LGPD.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided on our website.

7. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page. We encourage you to review it regularly.
